SLUICE — PIXEL ART ATLAS

Every piece of pixel art in the game, rendered live by the game's own draw functions (the hidden game boots below this page; nothing is duplicated). Solid cards = drawn art. Blue cards = live full-screen systems best viewed in-game. Dashed cards = planned, not drawn yet — the build-out worklist.
Bench page only — not part of the game build. Renderers are reached through window.__sluiceAtlas (js/sluice/378-art-atlas.js). A red card means that renderer threw when called standalone (usually it leans on live game state); open the console for the stack.
