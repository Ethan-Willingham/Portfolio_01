Every Push and Every Token

Every change I've made to this site, one dot each. These days I don't write the code, I just tell an AI what I want. The orange glow underneath is what that burns through in tokens.

scroll to zoom · drag to move · hover a dot to read it

Each dot is one change. Time goes left to right, and the higher a dot sits, the more it changed. Most of these I didn't write myself, I just told an AI what I wanted and it made the change. The orange glow along the bottom is the fuel: how many tokens that ate up, day by day. Scroll to zoom, drag to move around, hover a dot to see what it was.

Here's the part I think is neat: every one of these dots went straight to the live site. The AI and I just play ping-pong, change after change, and none of it ever sat in a draft. Every version of every post, the rough ones included, has been public the whole time.

The fuel

That glow runs on tokens, the little chunks of text an AI reads and writes to think. On the busiest day it chewed through more than a billion of them.
