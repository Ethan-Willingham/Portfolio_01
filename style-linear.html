How Long Can You Live?

The hard wall at 120 is unproven, the records are riddled with fraud, and the thing that actually adds years is the one nobody calls a hack.

There is supposed to be a hard wall around 120, and eating less is supposed to be the only real lever. The evidence says something stranger. The cleanest datasets keep showing a flat mortality hazard beyond the extremes, which is not what a hard limit would predict.
