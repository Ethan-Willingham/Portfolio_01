Five familiar treatments, each shown in the real masthead. All solid (no translucency), About is a quiet link (no pill), and the working results dropdown stays exactly as it is. Pick the one that feels most at home.

I studied the best-in-class patterns first: the consensus is restraint (an input, a placeholder, a magnifier, no shouting borders), the "/" shortcut hint the site already supports (Algolia DocSearch, GitHub, Linear), and the compact-that-expands idea you suggested (Trello-style).

Click into any field to feel its focus and grow behaviour. When one feels right, hit Choose and tell me the number. Nothing here is live or committed to the site.
